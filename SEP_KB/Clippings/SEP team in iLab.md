SEP in iLab:

Welcome to SEP in iLab, SEP teams selected are supported for 1 year subject to renewal.

SEP teams need to report progress at least once every 2 months- see Reporting Requirements - to maintain being in iLab’s SEP team.

Reporting requirements

First create a GitHub page describing your project/objectives/pitch deck, and other relevant info and progress

the GitHub page should be setup once your team is on board → inform (iLab- manager the link and it will be used by iLab to showcase your team and track progress.

E.g of creating pitch deck

In your Github, there should be section capturing progress - see below:

Progress: At least once every 2 months -

update via a 3~5 mins video/slides on YouTube → put this link in your Github progress section page

The first progress report = overview of your SEP objectives, team, etc

subsequent progress = progress in your dev works, etc

progress regarding - expenditure and deviations to finance/dev, investor’s activities, competitions, demos, etc

Non-reporting update (≥ 2 times) will result in team being disqualified from SEP.

2\. Example of how your GitHub page can look like: basically using GitHub as a place holder to keep on your information for reporting and your future angel investor to see progress
