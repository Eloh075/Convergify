Buy and Claim,
Reimbursement
(ICS)

Only one person in team to make purchases and do claims.

No purchases using digital currency.

ICS (Integrated Claim System) is for students to submit claims through Studentlink.

Submission of claims require upload of (1) invoices, (2) receipts, (3) bank statements, (4) expensed credits
records (if applicable), (5) email approval from iLab staff to make purchase.

Name your uploaded files in the format below when you upload your receipts and bank
statements (e.g., chatgpt-receipt-Apr25.pdf, chatgpt-google-bankstmt-Apr25.pdf).

Reimbursement is credited to your bank account registered with NTU.

After college has approved your claim, NTU Finance takes about 10 working days to fully process your claim.

Submit your claims monthly and not wait till end of FY.  Consolidated claims must not exceed $1000.00.

Do not double-claim.

Final claim for FY should be submitted a month before FY closing in mid-March.
