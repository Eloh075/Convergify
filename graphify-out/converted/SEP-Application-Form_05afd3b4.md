| Provide clear and tangible observations for a minimum of two existing competitors. Show how this project fares against them. Please provide a balanced view and state clearly on the areas that your approach works better and the areas where your competitors are better. (about 400 words)

Case 1: LinkedIn Learning
LinkedIn Learning offers a large proprietary course library tightly integrated with the LinkedIn professional network, where course certificates can be added directly to profiles for immediate visibility to employers. 
Convergify is content-agnostic: instead of relying on a single library, we aggregate learning resources across multiple platforms (e.g., YouTube, Coursera, SkillsFuture, MOOCs) and recommend them based on real-time job and skills demand, budget, and user skill level. This gives users more flexibility in where and how they upskill, while still being guided toward relevant programs.
Case 2: Jobscan & Teal HQ
Jobscan and Teal HQ primarily help candidates who already have target job postings by optimizing resumes and profiles to match those descriptions, including ATS keyword alignment, match scoring, and job-search organization tools. 

Convergify moves earlier in the timeline: we focus on detecting real-time skill gaps for target roles months before application and guiding users through upskilling, so they become stronger candidates rather than only tuning their resumes at the point of application.

Case 3: Steppingblocks
Steppingblocks aggregates large-scale alumni data to show where graduates work, what they earn, and which majors lead to specific roles, helping institutions and students evaluate long-term return on education and career pathways. 

Convergify analyses real-time job postings to track how skills needs change dynamically. Over time, as our user data grows, we can incorporate longitudinal outcomes into our prediction models while still remaining anchored in current demand.

Case 4: Singapore Government Tools (Career Kaki + MyCareersFuture)

Singapore government platforms like Career Kaki and MyCareersFuture provide verified job data via Singpass integration and SkillsFuture credits. They focus on conversational guidance and job discovery without structured skill gap analysis.

Convergify offers actionable outputs: real-time skill heatmaps from job sources, personalized learning roadmaps with course analysis, and resume analysis linking government data to application success. Unlike government tools that provide raw listings and subsidies, Convergify structures this data into a career playbook, helping mid-career workers and students utilize resources effectively.

Case 5: University Portals

University career portals like NUS Career+, NTU CareerAxis, etc provide specialized support for current students. However, access ends at graduation furthermore they lack cross-platform job aggregation, and offer no mid-career pivot support.

Convergify serves students through lifetime career pivots, aggregating live job data from platforms and generating SkillsFuture-compatible learning roadmaps that work beyond graduation. | Describe current status of your project. Have you received any funding to date? What potential industry partners and/or customers have you met with to validate your idea? What is the developing status? (about 200 words)
Current Development Status: We have developed a functional prototype of Convergify, a comprehensive job market intelligence platform that demonstrates significant technical achievement and market potential.

Core Platform Capabilities: 
Our prototype features six production-ready components: 
(1) An LLM-powered skill heatmap system that intelligently categorizes 273+ job roles using Google Gemini AI and visualizes skill demand across industries
(2) AI-driven resume parsing supporting multiple formats
(3) Multi-source job aggregation from 6 major job boards 
(4) UI for the Personalized career roadmaps with software engineering specializations
(5) Mockup of Application tracking system with interview scheduling
(6) A responsive React frontend with 40+ UI components.

Technical Infrastructure: The platform is built on a modern tech stack including Next.js 14, React 18, Supabase database, and Google Gemini AI integration. We've implemented comprehensive testing (Jest, property-based testing), RESTful APIs with CORS support, and database optimization with proper indexing. The system demonstrates scalable architecture ready for production deployment.

Data Integration: We've aggregated and processed job data from MyCareersFuture, Prosple, Glassdoor, Indeed, JobStreet, and eFinancialCareers, creating a robust dataset for skill analysis and market intelligence. |
